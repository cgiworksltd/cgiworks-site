(01) — The Studio

A studio of artists.

(02) — Who we are

CGI Works is an architectural visualisation studio. Our team of passionate artists comes from architectural and interior-design backgrounds — so we don't just render projects, we read them.

We work with branding and real-estate agencies, property developers, architects, interior designers and cultural institutions to create photo-real visualisations that highlight what's unique about each scheme.

(03) — How we work

We stay close to current design trends to keep your projects feeling fresh, memorable and inspiring — while making sure every brief lands in its own character, atmosphere and visual identity.

With close attention to composition, materials, lighting and detail, our aim is always the same: highly realistic, balanced, effective imagery that stands out and exceeds expectations.

(04) — Principles

What we value.

i.

Story first

Every image starts with the narrative. We compose around the idea — not the model.

ii.

Material truth

Textures, tones and reflectance modelled with conviction, not stock libraries.

iii.

Considered light

Lighting tuned to time of day, climate and mood — the cheapest, best storytelling tool.

iv.

Quiet detail

The detail you almost don't notice — that's what sells the image.

v.

Honest collaboration

We work as part of the design team, not adjacent to it. Critique welcomed.

vi.

On time, every time

Marketing deadlines and planning gateways are non-negotiable. So are we.

(02) — Process

How a project moves.

Most engagements move through four predictable stages, scaled to the size of the brief. We share visual previews at each gateway.

Stage 01.

Brief & cameras

We lock the storytelling angles, materials and lighting strategy with you before any pixels are pushed.

Stage 02.

Greyscale composition

Camera, composition and lighting locked in untextured greyscale — the cheapest place to make decisions.

Stage 03.

Texture & light

Full material pass, lighting tuning and atmosphere. Round of feedback at draft 01.

Stage 04.

Hero finish

Final styling, post-production and delivery in print and digital masters.

+

Variants

Once a hero is locked, additional cameras, seasons and time-of-day variants are produced efficiently.

+

Asset library

We retain a clean working scene so future updates — material swaps, new context, new staffage — are quick.

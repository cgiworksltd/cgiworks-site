Project · 2025

Tootal Buildings.

Category Heritage · Mixed-use
Year 2025
Location Manchester
Client Confidential developer
Sectors Heritage / Commercial / Public realm
(About the project)

An iconic Manchester landmark reimagined. We were commissioned to produce a sequence of context, façade-detail and public-realm visualisations capturing the careful restoration of the Tootal Buildings — one of the city's most recognisable Victorian silhouettes.

Each view is composed to emphasise the dialogue between the existing terracotta façade and the considered new public realm, with lighting tuned to the soft, overcast palette of north-west England.

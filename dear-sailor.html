Dear Sailor.

Category Hospitality · Bar & Restaurant
Year 2023
Location Manchester
Client Independent operator
Sectors Hospitality / Interior
(About the project)

Two interior CGIs developed in close partnership with the design team for an intimate bar and restaurant concept. The brief called for warmth, texture and a slightly cinematic edge — a place that already feels lived-in before the doors open.

Layered lighting, hand-aged materials and curated styling sell the atmosphere as much as the architecture.

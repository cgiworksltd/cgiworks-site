Product & Lifestyle.

Category Product · Lifestyle
Year 2025
Location Studio
Client Premium flooring brand
Sectors Product / Marketing
(About the project)

A series of styled living-room visualisations developed for a premium flooring collection. Each scene swaps in a different finish — chevron, herringbone, shaker, plank — while keeping a consistent furniture language and light condition, so the product itself stays the hero.

Carefully art-directed reflectance, micro-texture and natural light give every finish its own character without breaking the family.

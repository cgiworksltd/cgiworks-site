Project · 2025

The Workplace Suite.

Category Commercial · Amenity
Year 2025
Location United Kingdom
Client Confidential
Sectors Workplace / Wellbeing / Amenity
(About the project)

A suite of five amenity-led interior CGIs supporting the marketing campaign for a new commercial building — reception arrivals, open-plan workspace, gym and changing rooms. The work showcases a building positioned around wellbeing, light and ease.

Coordinated palette, consistent staffage logic and shared lighting strategy unify the set across two distinct camera languages: cinematic wide-angle reception views and tighter, editorial amenity vignettes.

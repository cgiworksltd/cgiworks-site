Project · 2025

Trinity Residence.

Category Interior · Residential
Year 2025
Location London
Client Private
Sectors High-end residential
(About the project)

A trio of refined interior visualisations for a private residential commission — kitchen, ensuite and principal bathroom — produced to support the design narrative through final-stage client approvals.

Tonal restraint, hand-selected materials and quiet, naturalistic light give each room a calm sense of permanence. Surfaces, joinery and reflectance were modelled with close consultation to the design team.

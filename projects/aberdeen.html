Aberdeen Residence.

CategoryInterior · Residential
Year2025
LocationAberdeen, Scotland
ClientPrivate
SectorsHigh-end residential
(About the project)

A complete-floor residential commission set in a refined Scottish stone townhouse. We produced bathroom, bedroom, living-room and exterior visualisations to support the design team through approvals and sales material.

Each room balances contemporary furniture choices against the building's classical envelope — marble, oak, brass and warm textiles photographed in soft northern daylight.
